About
AI Engineer and Full Stack Developer with 4+ years of engineering experience shipping production web applications and AI-powered products. Currently building GenAI features at Kaitongo — RAG pipelines, agent-based workflows, semantic search, and LLM integrations for a B2B intelligence platform.
My AI journey started with conversational AI (an AI-powered travel chatbot at Avathi), grew through document-processing pipelines at Metapercept (a foundation of today's RAG systems), and is now focused deeply on building production LLM systems at Kaitongo.
🤖 AI Engineering Focus
- LLMs (Gemini, GPT, Claude) — integration, evaluation, production deployment
- RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) — chunking, embeddings, re-ranking
- AI Agents & Agent-based Workflows — ReAct, function calling, multi-step reasoning
- LangChain — chains, tools, memory, LCEL
- Vector Search — OpenSearch, Weaviate, semantic retrieval
- Prompt Engineering — few-shot, CoT, structured output, guardrails
- Conversational AI & Chatbot Development
- Streaming UIs with SSE for real-time AI responses
⚡ Full Stack Strengths
- Frontend: React.js, Next.js, Vue.js, Nuxt.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Redux, Vuex
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js, FastAPI, Python, MongoDB, REST APIs
- DevOps: AWS (Lambda, EC2, S3), Docker, CI/CD, Vercel, Git
💼 Where I've Built
- Kaitongo (Current) — LLM-powered B2B intelligence platform with RAG, agents, semantic search
- Avathi — Travel booking platform with Channex + HyperGuest integrations and an AI-powered chatbot
- Metapercept — Vue.js + React.js frontends for document migration SaaS with content-processing pipelines
- Prism Infoways — MERN stack full-stack applications
🎯 What I'm Looking For
AI Engineer, GenAI Engineer, LLM Engineer, or AI-focused Full Stack roles where I can ship real AI products — not just demos. Particularly interested in RAG systems, agent architectures, and teams building tooling that makes AI actually work in production.
📫 Open to roles across India + Remote opportunities globally.
